Paste a service

Paste an entire service in the format below. # starts a song, > a scripture, ! a note. Round-trips: Use my current service exports the live service in this format so you can edit and re-paste.

Format key
  • # Title — song. Next line under the title is the attribution (optional). Blank line separates stanzas. [chorus] on its own line marks the chorus stanza.
  • > Reference — scripture. Optional ~ Translation on the next line. One verse per line, starting with the verse number.
  • ! Title — note. Body paragraphs separated by blank lines.
